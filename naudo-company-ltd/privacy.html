Data Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 22, 2025

NAUDO COMPANY LIMITED is committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal data. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect the information you provide to us through our website and services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information primarily in two ways:

  • Direct Submissions: When you use our contact form, we collect your **Name**, **Email Address**, and **Message/Inquiry Details**.
  • Automated Data: We may collect non-identifiable data such as your IP address, browser type, and pages visited, used solely for analytics and improving our website performance.

2. Use of Your Information

The information submitted via the contact form is used exclusively for:

  • Responding to your consultation requests, inquiries, or quotes.
  • Communicating with you about our services or products.
  • Internal record-keeping and business analysis.

Your data is **never** sold or rented to third parties.

3. Data Storage and Security

We are a technology company and take data security seriously. We implement robust, industry-standard security measures, including encryption and access controls, to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. All form submissions are handled securely.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We will not share your personal data with any third party, except:

  • With your explicit consent.
  • To comply with legal obligations (e.g., a court order).
  • To trusted service providers who assist us in operating our business (e.g., email service), provided they adhere to strict confidentiality agreements.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, rectification of, or erasure of your personal data held by NAUDO. Please direct any such requests to Support@naudoltd.com.

6. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
